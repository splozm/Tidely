# Tidely – Product Guide

## What is Tidely?

Tidely is an Android app that shows UK tide times at a glance — directly on your home screen. No opening an app, no searching, no faff. The next high tide and next low tide are always visible on your home screen widget.

---

## Who is it for?

Anyone who needs to know the tides quickly:
- Beachgoers
- Surfers
- Coastal walkers
- Fishermen
- Boaters and sailors
- Dog walkers on tidal beaches
- Anyone living near the UK coast

---

## Core Features

### Home Screen Widget
- Shows next high tide — time and height in metres
- Shows next low tide — time and height in metres
- Shows whether tide is currently rising or falling
- Updates automatically throughout the day
- Works offline once data is fetched

### Main App
- Full tide table for today and the next 6 days
- Visual tide curve graph
- Nearest station auto-detected via GPS
- Manually search and pin any of 607 UK tidal stations
- Heights shown in metres

### Location
- Auto-detects nearest UK tidal station using device GPS
- User can override and select any station manually
- Remembers last selected station

---

## What it is not

- Not a navigation tool — not for use at sea
- Not a weather app
- Not a real-time sensor — predictions are astronomical, not live measurements

## Data Source

Tidal predictions sourced from the ADMIRALTY UK Tidal API, operated by the UK Hydrographic Office. 607 tidal stations across the United Kingdom. Data is authoritative and accurate for leisure use.
